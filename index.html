How to use

  • Begin by adding all the tasks you want to accomplish on today’s session.
  • If something is too complex it might help to break it down into a multiple steps.
  • Try to imagine how much time each step will take to keep your goals realistic!
  • Pick a sound you enjoy, It will help you keep focus and minimize any distractions.
  • After completing four pomodoros, you should take a longer break.
  • You can also set a custom timer, but remember to get enough time to rest ♥

How it works

POMODORO TECHNIQUE

The Pomodoro technique is a time management method that uses a timer to divide work into focused 25-minute intervals separated by short breaks. This method helps improve focus and productivity by reducing distractions and allowing for regular breaks.

BACKGROUND SOUNDS

Using background sounds can enhance your focus by creating a calming environment and minimizing external distractions. This can be especially helpful in busy work or study spaces.
